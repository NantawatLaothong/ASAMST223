Introduction

 The Asian American community has a rich and diverse history in the United States, yet their contributions and struggles are often overlooked or erased in mainstream narratives of American history. To address this gap, a project has been initiated to create a comprehensive timeline of Asian American history, highlighting key events, individuals, and movements that have shaped the community's experiences and identities over time. The purpose of this project is to raise awareness and promote understanding of Asian American history, as well as to celebrate the community's achievements and resilience in the face of adversity. By creating a timeline that spans from the earliest Asian immigrants to the present day, this project aims to provide a comprehensive and accessible resource for educators, scholars, and the general public to learn about Asian American history and its significance in shaping the fabric of American society.
